ABOUT OUR VILLAS

Whether you are a regular visitor to Bawley Point or making your way here for the first time, our five architecturally designed luxury villas and our Ocean View Villa offer everything you need to feel at home from the moment you arrive. Positioned on our beautiful property to take advantage of a north easterly aspect and tastefully furnished to provide you with comfort and convenience, they are guaranteed to have you wishing you could stay for longer.

All of the villas are fully self-contained, with all of the modern conveniences necessary to make your stay relaxing and stress-free. The beds are ready for your arrival with full bed linen and bath and beach towels are supplied as are complimentary toiletries. The kitchens are stocked with all of the implements you need to prepare a delicious meal, which you can enjoy in the spacious lounge/dining area or out on the generous deck while you soak up the peacefulness of the bush. If barbequing is more your style, then all you will need to do is turn on the gas on your Weber or Beefeater BBQ and you will be ready to grill in no time at all.

We are the perfect accommodation for all travellers, including ones who like to take their furry companions with them when they venture from home. If this describes you, please enquire with us about accommodating your pet. For a modest additional charge we accept some small to medium size pets on the property and within the cottages when they are under the care and the supervision of their owners.
